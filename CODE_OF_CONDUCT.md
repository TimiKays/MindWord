# Community Guidelines (English)

Just a few simple rules for happy communication:

## Basic Principles

- **Be friendly**: Discuss issues calmly, no arguments
- **Help each other**: Be patient with beginner questions  
- **Respect differences**: Everyone has different usage habits, be understanding

## Unwelcome Behavior

These behaviors are not welcome:
- Personal attacks, insults
- Malicious spamming
- Discriminatory remarks
- Deliberately spreading misinformation

## See a Problem?

- You can directly point out inappropriate behavior to me
- I'll handle it based on the situation, may include deleting content, warnings, etc.
- Serious cases may result in direct blocking

## Disclaimer

I'm an individual developer with limited time, may not be able to handle all issues promptly, but will try my best to maintain a friendly communication environment.
